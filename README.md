# Tata Online Retail Performance Analysis

## Project Overview
This analysis examines Tataâ€™s online retail performance to uncover trends in revenue, customer behavior, product demand, and geographic contribution. The dashboard provides a clear view of key metrics and insights to support business decision-making.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Data Cleaning & Transformation
- Data Visualization
- Sales & Customer Analytics

## Objectives
- Analyze overall revenue and customer activity
- Identify high-performing products and markets
- Detect seasonal trends in sales
- Highlight key customers contributing to revenue

## Key KPIs
- **Total revenue:** Exceeded 9M, reflecting strong sales performance  
- **Orders:** 25,900 from 4,373 customers, indicating solid engagement  
- **Items sold:** Over 5 million, showing high product demand  
- **Revenue per customer:** 373.56, highlighting consistent spending behavior  

## Key Findings
- The **UK** emerges as the top market by revenue and customer count, followed by the **Netherlands, Eire, Germany, and France**  
- Revenue **increases steadily from January**, peaks in **November**, and remains strong in **December**, reflecting seasonal purchasing patterns  
- A **small group of top products and high-value customers** contributes a significant share of total revenue  

## Business Impact
These insights can guide decisions around:
- **Customer retention strategies**  
- **Inventory planning**  
- **Targeted marketing campaigns**  

## Conclusion
The analysis highlights strong sales performance, clear seasonality, and concentrated value from key markets and customers. Understanding these trends can help optimize revenue growth and business strategy.
